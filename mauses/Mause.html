Razer Ouroboros Elite Ambidextrous Gaming

Razer Ouroboros is equipped with a laser sensor 4G (fourth generation). The mouse can be adjusted to a specific surface and adjust the height of the mouse's detachment from the surface. A brand-new trigger switching trigger allows you to temporarily reduce or increase DPI, depending on the game situation, you can quickly move from high sensitivity for movement in attack to low sensitivity for sniper mode and back. With Razer Ouroboros, you get an absolute modern performance standard for pro gamers for any game you play - whether it's a first-person shooter, real-time strategy or role-playing game.

Razer Ouroboros has an ultra-low delay of 1 ms in the wireless mode of the game class, the reaction speed of the mouse is exactly the same as in the wired mode. With this phenomenal, universal, deadly game mouse, the victory will be easy.

Razer Naga Trinity

Razer Naga Trinity is the ninth iteration of the popular Razer Naga family and is a modular mouse with three removable side panels. The first is the 12-button panel from Razer Naga Chroma, intended for mass games like World of Warcraft. The total number of programmable buttons in this case is 19 pieces. The second panel from Razer Naga Hex V2 is equipped with a 7-button wheel and is perfect for Heroes of the Storm, Dota 2 and Diablo III. Finally, the third panel has only two buttons and is designed for everyday use.
